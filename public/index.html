Wave Digital Filters are a mathematical framework for modeling analog circuits digitally. Unlike traditional DSP approximations, WDF models the actual physical behavior of circuit components (resistors, capacitors, op-amps) by simulating how waves propagate through them. This results in more accurate, stable, and musically pleasing emulations of analog gear.
