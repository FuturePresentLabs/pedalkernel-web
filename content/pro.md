## PedalKernel Pro

While the **PedalKernel kernel** is open source and free for personal use, **PedalKernel Pro** is how we work with companies who want to build commercial products on our technology.

We partner with hardware manufacturers, plugin developers, and audio companies to license the PedalKernel WDF engine for their products.

## What We Offer

### For Plugin Developers

White-label VST3, CLAP, AU, and AAX plugins built on the PedalKernel kernel. You bring the circuits or choose from our reference library — we handle the DSP, UI, and packaging.

Our partner **[Puget Audio](https://puget.audio/)** distributes consumer-facing plugins under the **Puget Legends** brand — a collection of meticulously modeled classic pedals and studio processors.

[Explore Puget Legends →](https://puget.audio/plugins/legends/)

### For Hardware Manufacturers

DSP implementations for pedal and amp manufacturers. The same WDF circuits running on embedded processors — STM32, XMOS, FPGAs, or your target platform.

Whether you're building a digital multi-effects unit or modeling amps for a hybrid design, we can port and optimize our circuits for your hardware.

- Circuit modeling from schematics
- Porting existing algorithms to WDF
- Integration with your existing codebase
- Performance optimization (SIMD, parallel trees)

## The Technology

**Wave Digital Filters** simulate circuits at the component level — resistors, capacitors, op-amps, transistors — preserving the physical relationships that create "that sound."

- **Dynamic response** — Circuits behave differently at different volumes
- **Interactive controls** — Knobs affect the signal path realistically  
- **Non-linear behavior** — Saturation and clipping happen naturally
- **Stability guaranteed** — Won't blow up under any conditions

Built in **Rust** for memory safety without garbage collection. Zero-allocation audio paths. Real-time safe by design.
